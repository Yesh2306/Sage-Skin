Skin Awareness & Protection

Your skin acts as a protective barrier against pollution, UV radiation, bacteria, and dehydration. Damage to this barrier can lead to acne, pigmentation, premature aging, and infections.

  • ✔ Use sunscreen daily to prevent sun damage & skin cancer risk
  • ✔ Avoid harsh scrubs that weaken skin barrier
  • ✔ Keep skin hydrated to maintain elasticity
  • ✔ Seek medical advice for persistent acne or irritation
  • ✔ Annual skin check-ups help detect early skin conditions

At Sage & Skin, we conduct awareness sessions on acne management, sun safety, anti-aging prevention, and skin hygiene practices for all age groups.

Basic Dermatologist Recommended Routine

Morning Routine 🌞

  • Step 1: Gentle cleanser
  • Step 2: Antioxidant serum (Vitamin C)
  • Step 3: Lightweight moisturizer
  • Step 4: Broad spectrum SPF 30+

Night Routine 🌙

  • Step 1: Cleanser / Double cleanse
  • Step 2: Prescribed treatment serum
  • Step 3: Moisturizer
  • Step 4: Adequate sleep (7-8 hrs)

Weekly Care 💧

  • Mild exfoliation (1-2 times/week)
  • Hydrating mask
  • Avoid over-experimenting with products
